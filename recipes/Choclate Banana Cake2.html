Banana cake with Choclate
Choclate Banana Cake

For the cake:

  • Ripe bananas (mashed)
  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Buttermilk or milk
  • Chocolate chips or chunks (optional)

For the frosting:

  • Cream cheese
  • Unsalted butter
  • Powdered sugar
  • Vanilla extract

instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 13x9 inch pan.

Step 2: In a mixing bowl, combine the cake mix, mashed bananas, oil, and eggs. Use an electric mixer on low speed and beat for 30 seconds.

Step 3: Increase the mixer speed to medium and beat for an additional 2 minutes.

Step 4: Pour the batter into the prepared pan, spreading it evenly.

Step 5: Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 33 to 36 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.

Step 6: Allow the cake to cool completely.

Step 7: Frost the cake with your choice of frosting, if desired.
