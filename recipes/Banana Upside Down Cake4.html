Banana Upside Down Cake

Yes, Upside Down
Banana Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup chopped pecans
  • 4 bananas, sliced
  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package banana cake mix

instructions

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 2 (8 inch) pans.

Step 2: Combine butter and brown sugar, divide and spread evenly between the 2 pans. Sprinkle pecans evenly over both brown sugar mixtures, arrange banana slices evenly over each.

Step 3: Prepare cake mix according to package instructions. Divide batter into the 2 pans.

Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven for 40 to 45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.

Step 5: Turn cakes upside down on a platter or plate, gently tap the bottom and carefully remove pans, replacing caramel mixture that sticks to the pan.
